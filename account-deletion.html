Zelli Salon App – Account Deletion Request

To delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to hello.zellisalonapp@gmail.com from the email registered with your account.
  2. Include your full name, registered email, and specify whether you are a Customer or Salon Owner.

Data Deleted

Upon your request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account login credentials
  • Customer booking history
  • Salon profile and services (for salon owners)
  • Uploaded images and documents

Data Retained

Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for internal reporting for up to 12 months.

Once the request is processed, we will confirm via email.
